Josicon Engineering Plc Company

is established as a sole proprietor in 2009 E.C. owned and managed by Josicon Engineering Plc and its family, registered, and licensed as B.C. contractors by trade and Industry Development Bureau of Addis Ababa city Administration having construction license of Building contractor, which is renewed for currently facial year.

The firm is also registered for VAT by Federal Inland Revenue Authority and taxpayer identification number given from the same authority. In addition, the company has domestic investment license obtained from the Investment Bureau of Addis Ababa city Administration, for total capital investment of 1.2 mi.

Currently the company is engaged on construction of civil projects mostly on Building construction projects. On its early beginning in the construction sector, the company is gaining a good reputation from its client based on the previously completed projects.
